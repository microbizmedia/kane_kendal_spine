Lower Back Pain Treatment in Montgomery

Lower back pain is one of the most common conditions affecting adults and can make everyday activities more difficult. It may be caused by muscle strain, ligament sprain, disc problems, or irritation in the joints and nerves of the spine. At Kane & Kendall Spine Management, we provide trusted, personalized care to help relieve pain, restore mobility, and improve your quality of life.

What Is Lower Back Pain?

Lower back pain refers to discomfort in the lumbar spine, which supports much of your upper body weight. It can result from injuries, degenerative conditions, poor posture, or everyday strain, often involving muscles, ligaments, discs, or joints.

Treatment & Prevention

If your pain does not improve with basic care, our experienced team offers advanced, minimally invasive treatments such as epidural steroid injections, radiofrequency neurotomy, targeted nerve injections, and SI joint treatments. We also help prevent future pain through posture correction, exercise guidance, and healthy daily habits.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are common causes of lower back pain?

Lower back pain is often caused by muscle strain, ligament injuries, herniated discs, arthritis, poor posture, or excess weight. Injuries from sports or accidents can also contribute.

When should I see a doctor?

You should see a doctor if your pain lasts more than a few weeks, worsens, or includes symptoms like numbness, weakness, or loss of bladder or bowel control.

What treatments are available?

We offer targeted treatments including epidural steroid injections, radiofrequency neurotomy, transforaminal injections, and SI joint injections based on your condition.

How can I prevent future back pain?

Maintaining a healthy weight, staying active, practicing good posture, lifting properly, and avoiding smoking can significantly reduce the risk of future back pain.

Are injections safe?

Yes, when performed by experienced specialists, injections are a safe and effective minimally invasive option to reduce inflammation and relieve pain.
