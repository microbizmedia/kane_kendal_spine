Headache & Migraine Relief in Montgomery

Headaches affect millions of people and can range from occasional discomfort to chronic, severe pain that disrupts daily life. Many patients also experience tension, pressure, or even nausea. At Kane & Kendall Spine Management, we focus on identifying the root cause of your headaches so we can provide lasting relief—not just temporary solutions.

What Causes Headaches?

Most headaches—including tension headaches, migraines, and cluster headaches—are not caused by disease, but by imbalances in the body. Poor posture, muscle tension, and spinal misalignment can all contribute to pressure and irritation in the neck and surrounding nerves, leading to recurring headaches.

Treatment & Prevention

Our approach focuses on reducing tension and correcting underlying issues that may be triggering your headaches. Treatment may include chiropractic adjustments, targeted therapies, and lifestyle guidance to improve posture, reduce stress, and support long-term relief. Preventative care helps reduce the frequency and intensity of future headaches.

Why Choose Kane & Kendall Spine Management

We provide personalized, hands-on care designed to treat the source of your pain. Our team is committed to helping you feel better through safe, effective treatments in a supportive and comfortable environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of headaches do you treat?

We treat tension headaches, migraines, and cluster headaches, as well as headaches caused by neck tension and spinal misalignment.

What causes frequent headaches?

Common causes include stress, poor posture, muscle tension, dehydration, and misalignment in the neck and spine.

Can chiropractic care help migraines?

Yes. Chiropractic care can reduce tension, improve spinal alignment, and relieve pressure on nerves that may contribute to migraines.

How can I prevent headaches?

Maintaining good posture, staying hydrated, managing stress, and receiving regular care can help reduce headache frequency.

When should I seek treatment?

If headaches are frequent, severe, or affecting your daily life, it’s best to seek professional evaluation to identify the cause.

Do you accept new patients?

Yes, we welcome new patients and offer personalized care plans based on your needs.
